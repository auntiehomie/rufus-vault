# BankBud

A personal finance app that uses AI to improve a user's knowledge and understanding of personal finance from multiple angles. Users can read news about banking, crypto, savings, and personal finance, see rates from local and online banks, and chat with BankBud - an AI assistant that learns about the user over time to give more personalized financial guidance.
